Ever wanted to watch a new anime or manga, but had no idea where to look?

Well, we've got your back!

Our chatbot provides an interactive and intuitive way for users to express their preferences and interests in genres, authors and more, by guiding users through a series of questions while providing suggestions as answers are typed.

We believe that our chatbot not only simplifies the process of gathering user preferences, but also enhances the discovery of new content by offering relevant and personalized results. It's designed to make the experience interactive, engaging, and user-friendly, ultimately helping users find their next favorite anime or manga series.

Website interface:

Below are drop down menus containing information on each of our website's features:

Hamburger icon (register, history, settings, etc)

So on the left-hand side of this screen, you'll see an icon with 3 lines (a hamburger button). In it, we have

Register

The register feature allows users to create accounts to save their history and user preferences from the settings button.

History

The history feature allows users to view their past interactions and regenerate results using their past inputs.

Settings

In our settings, users are able to change their passwords and theme to their liking, using colors of their choice.
These features are also only available for users who are logged in.

Sign in

After registering users can sign into their accounts, which will then show their chosen color preferences and saved history. The sign in button also disappears once users are logged in.

Home button

The home button returns back to the start page regardless of where you are in the website.

Quiz features

Upon clicking the start button, users will be prompted with questions requiring an input. Asides from the first question, users have the ability to choose to go back to the previous question and change their answers, or even skip the questions to leave them unanswered.
While there is an autocomplete function, clicking the next button validates the user's inputs to make sure they are valid. Clicking the skip button moves onto the next question regardless of what you've typed in though!
